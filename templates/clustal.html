Clustal-OMICS: A Clustal Omega executable

Clustal-Omega is a powerful tool for multiple sequence alignment, helping you align sequences of DNA, RNA, and protein. With the Clustal-OMICS web application, the process is made even easier.

You can input your data in a variety of ways, including fasta sequences, Uniprot IDs, or a fasta file. With just a few clicks, you'll be able to run Clustal-Omega and visualize the aligned sequences in your desired format.

Clustal-OMICS has been developed to save you time and effort. Instead of having to download and install the software or run the program from the command line, you can simply upload your data and get results instantly.

Additionally, Clustal-OMICS is user-friendly and accessible, even for those without prior experience in bioinformatics. The intuitive interface makes it easy for you to align your sequences without having to worry about complex command line arguments or confusing software settings.

Clustal-OMICS tutorial

1) Select your input method. You can choose to enter fasta sequences, Uniprot IDs, or upload a fasta file.

2) Enter or upload your data as appropriate. If entering fasta sequences, make sure they are formatted correctly.

3) Once you have inputted your data, you can select the desired output format. Clustal-OMICS supports visualizing the aligned sequences and downloading the results as a file.

4) Click the "Run Clustal-Omega" button to start the alignment process.

5) Wait for the results to be generated. This may take a few minutes, depending on the size of your input data.

6) Once the results are generated, you can visualize the aligned sequences or download the results as a file.

With Clustal-OMICS, aligning sequences has never been easier!
